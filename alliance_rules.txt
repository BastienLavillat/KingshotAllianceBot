Alliance Rules
* Moderators are authorized to enforce these rules and may apply penalties to members who violate them
* Not knowing the rules doesn't excuse breaking them — it's each member's responsibility to read and understand them
* The rules may evolve over time. If that's the case you'll be notified of it through the [annoucement](https://discord.com/channels/1516079671639408873/1521536467648909453) channel
---
Rule 1 - Respect the NAP (Non-Aggression Pact)
* This pact applies to the Top 6 Alliances in our Kingdom and their academies:
  * PowerAlliance (VIP) — Academy: GalacticEmpire (UFO)
  * TheCrimsonOrder (XII) — Academy: XIIACADEMY (300)
  * Bloodhound (NYX) — Academy: ABN (ABN)
  * NUSANTARA (NST) — Academy: AMSsumsel (NsT)
  * LaFamilia (LFP) — Academy: LaFamiliaPT (LFA)
  * FriendlyUNion (FUN) — Academy: WorldofWalkers (FUn)
* The following actions against any member of these alliances are strictly prohibited:
  * Scouting/Attacking cities (1:1 retaliation strike allowed)
  * Attacking gatherers
  * Actively recruiting members from other alliances
* If a player chooses to switch alliances on their own initiative, this does not constitute active recruitment. However, the accepting alliance is expected to notify the player's original alliance as a courtesy
* Any NAP violation must be reported and documented. The offending player, their alliance, and the NAP council will all be notified accordingly
* Violations are handled progressively based on severity and frequency:
  * 1st offense — Warning
  * 2nd offense — Demotion
  * 3rd offense — Kick temporarily (appeal window: 3 to 30 days, at the NAP council's discretion)
  * 4th offense and beyond — Ban from any NAP alliance
---
Rule 2 — No Insults or Bullying
* Your behavior in-game reflects on the entire alliance. Be mindful of how you communicate, avoid taking things personally, and exercise restraint when dealing with difficult players.
* Most people play this game to have fun — keep that in mind. Repeatedly and freely attacking other players or alliances without cause is not acceptable.
* If you have an issue with another player, follow these steps in order:
  1. Attempt to resolve the situation through calm, respectful dialogue
  2. If that fails, disengage and ignore the player
  3. If the situation persists and becomes impossible to ignore, reach out to an R4 or R5 for assistance
