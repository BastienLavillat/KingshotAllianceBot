# Discord Server Rules
* In addition to these rules, all members must follow Discord's [Terms](https://discord.com/terms) of Service and [Community Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines)
* Moderators are authorized to enforce these rules and may apply penalties to members who violate them
* Not knowing the rules doesn't excuse breaking them — it's each member's responsibility to read and understand them
* Consider these rules to apply to other communication channels such as Telegram or in-game chat
* The rules may evolve over time. If that's the case you'll be notified through the [annoucement](https://discord.com/channels/1516079671639408873/1521536467648909453) channel
---
# Rule 1 — Be civil, respectful and helpful
* Treat all members with respect — no one is superior to anyone else in this server
* If someone asks a question you find basic, take the time to help them rather than being dismissive or condescending
* Topics such as religion, sexuality and politics can spark strong disagreements. If you're not able to engage with differing opinions calmly and respectfully, please refrain from participating in those discussions
* This is not a dating server. Keep interactions appropriate and be mindful of other members' comfort
---
# Rule 2 — Stick to the channel topic
* Keep your messages relevant to the channel you're posting in. Each channel has a description — if you're unsure what belongs there, take a moment to read it before posting
---
# Rule 3 — The moderation is always right
* You are welcome to respectfully challenge a moderator's decision, and the team is always open to hearing your perspective. However, once a final decision has been reached, it is to be respected and accepted by all members
---
# Rule 4 — We don't talk about the fight club
* That's all. Thank you very much for taking the time to read these rules
* And we don't talk about the fight club
